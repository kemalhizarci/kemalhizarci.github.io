this is where i tell you who’s behind this corner of the internet.

my name is kemal hızarcı.
i’m an interior architecture student, visual artist, and occasional code tinkerer.
born in 2003 / based between niksar and istanbul.

currently studying at mimar sinan fine arts university, focused on the intersection of space, memory, and interaction.

my work includes:

  • interactive design & web-based installations
  • zero-waste retail and urban commons
  • walking as spatial method
  • visual archives & pixel decay

some projects:

  • an imaginary line made by walking (2024) — kadıköy, istanbul
  • zima blue grid (2025) — tile-based game with p5.js
  • zero-waste supermarket design (2025) — suadiye
